Initial release: a Flutter AppBar-style header with two variants — `bar`
(fixed top bar: back button, title, icon actions with loading spinners) and
`title` (in-flow page title: large bold title, description, icon actions with
badge support). Self-contained injected styles, `--ax-header-*` tokens with
`--sidebar-width`/`--text-color` fallbacks, responsive + reduced-motion +
high-contrast rules, and per-part `classNames` overrides.
