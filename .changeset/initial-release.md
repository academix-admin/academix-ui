Initial public release.

- `@academix/navigation-stack`: client-side navigation stack with transitions,
  swipe-back, scroll restoration, lazy pages, nested/group navigation,
  cross-page dependency injection and a request/response bus.
- `@academix/state-stack`: framework-agnostic cross-tab state management with
  IndexedDB-first persistence, BroadcastChannel sync, undo/redo, TTL, atoms and
  route-scoped demand state, plus an optional Next.js router adapter at
  `@academix/state-stack/next`.
